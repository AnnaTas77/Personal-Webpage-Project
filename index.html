BIO

While my background is in environmental conservation and administration, my passion for technology, creating things from scratch, and solving problems in unique and creative ways inspired my journey towards software development.

What excites me about software development is the ability to solve complex problems, turn ideas into functional applications, and create user-friendly experiences.

Coming from an environmental protection background, I recognize the increasing importance of software development in achieving sustainability goals. I'm thrilled about the prospect of combining my passion for technology with my commitment to environmental protection in my future career.

The ever-evolving nature of software development is also particularly appealing to me as it ensures that I'll constantly encounter new and exciting challenges and opportunities to explore emerging technologies and trends.

Additionally, I appreciate the collaborative nature of software development. Working in teams to bring ideas to life through code aligns well with my values.

In summary, my transition to software development embodies my passion for technology and my enthusiasm for a career that continually challenges and inspires me.
